Shelter for a family

One house for eight souls. Actually, only one room hosts Faisal Al Yafaei and his family. A room that doesn’t protect them from neither summer heat nor cold of winter. And because the walls are incomplete, Faisal used sheets to put a roof over his family’s head. On each wall, clothes are hanging. In the back of the room there is a small place to cook. When it rains, the rain passes through the sheets and the whole room becomes wet.
